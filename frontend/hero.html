About Us

Where great teachers meet bright classrooms.

GleeKids was established in 2022 in Talisay City, Negros Occidental. We connect office-based and home-based Online ESL Teachers with institutions that need dependable, high-quality teaching talent.

Our Mission

Fulfilling careers, top-tier teaching talent.

Empower educators with fulfilling careers and provide institutions with top-tier teaching talent through careful recruitment, warm support, and a community that helps teachers grow.
